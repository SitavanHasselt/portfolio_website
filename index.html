BIO

Hi, there! My name is Sita and I like learning new things and making/creating stuff. Currently focussed on documentary filmmaking I feel passionate about storytelling to discover new perspectives and building connections by sharing experiences. In my work I try to be refreshing and honest, and with a lot of attention for authenticity.

Based in Barcelona I have been developing my audio-visual skills with OTOXO Productions as a videographer and co-director on the television documentary Bicibús: Pedals Endavant which is set to air on Barcelona’s TV-Network Beteve this fall.For my latest project I am now in the process of researching the ‘pueblos abandonados’ here in the north of Spain.

Before transitioning into filmmaking I studied Physics at Delft University of Technology. Coming from , the world that we live in is amazingly complex and deserves attention and careful consideration. I try to bring this curious and analytical outlook with me in all aspects of my life including my filmmaking.

I’m flattered that you’ve taken interest in me and I would love to learn more about you! I am always interested in meeting new people and open to collaborating, so if you want to get in contact leave me a message here.
