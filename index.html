BIO

Hi, there! My name is Sita, I'm a curious researcher and documentary filmmaker with a passion for empowering stories and rural places. I use my camera to tell honest and refreshing stories about local change-makers and .... .

Currently based in Barcelona I have been working with OTOXO Productions, improving my audio-visual skills and being part of two documentary productions: Bicibús and La Subversiva. As a co-director I'm excited to announce that Bicibús will air on Betevé early 2023.

My background in physics has helped me shape a technical and analytical mindset. Connecting the abstract data to its human form has form the basis of a my research and storytelling.

I'm always excited to get involved in interesting projects and new collaborations so please get in touch!

PROJECTS

La Subversiva [In Production]

directing | editing

Undermining big, capitalist beer brands La Subversiva is Barcelona's joint force artisinal brewery. Sticking to their activist and anarchist principles their mission is to turn everybody's favourite social drink into a truly, social drink.

La Subersiva
  • Release: November 2022
  • Languages: Catalan
  • Subtitles: English, Dutch
  • Produced by: OTOXO Productions
  • Directed & Edited by: Sita van Hasselt

In collaboration with La Subversiva, find out more on subversiva.coop.

Synopsis:

La Subersiva was born out of the desire to make Producing beer for Barcelona's underground social movements and returning its profits back into its origins they have formed a perfect circular economy. Opening their new factory has allowed them to acces a wider clientel but the monthly credit payments form a bit of a bottle neck, will they be able to make it work?

Bicibús: Pedals Endavant

co-directing | videography

Bicibús is a social documentary produced by OTOXO Productions. The film follows a group of parents and volunteers in the neighborhood Eixample as they set out to reclaim the streets from heavy traffic. Watch how Barcelona cracks a smile as the joyful bicibús passes by.

Bicibús: Pedals Endavant
  • Release: Oktober 2022
  • Running time:
  • Languages: Spanish, Catalan
  • Subtitles: English
  • Produced by: OTOXO Productions
  • Directed by: John English, Tom Garner, Rosie Williams, Jenny Dinwoodie, Esme Warren, Caitlin Long, Andy Perez, Clara Cabajo, Katja Kuivanen, Oliver Gibel, Mattia Bullo, Sofia Luz, Alejandra Angulo Alonso, Anya Field, Andra Ion & Sita van Hasselt
Synopsis:

OTOXO Productions' 2022 documentary, 'Bicibús'. The film follows a group of Barcelona parents and children who have joined together to create a stronger community and safer bicycling conditions for the city - starting a local bicibus. The parade of cycling families take the streets every Friday morning, creating a joyful and eco-friendly route to Barcelona elementary schools.
