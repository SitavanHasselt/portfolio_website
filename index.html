BIO

Hi, there! My name is Sita, I'm a curious researcher and documentary filmmaker with a passion for telling honest and empowering stories about local change-makers.

Currently based in Barcelona I have been working with OTOXO Productions, improving my audio-visual skills and being part of two documentary productions: Bicibús and La Subversiva. As a co-director I'm excited to announce that Bicibús will air on Betevé early 2023.

My background in physics has helped me shape a technical and analytical mindset. "Connecting abstract data to its human significance has formed the basis of my research and storytelling."

I'm always excited to get involved in interesting projects and available for freelance or contracted work, so please get in touch!

La Subversiva

Undermining big, capitalist beer brands La Subversiva is Barcelona's joint force artisanal brewery. Sticking to their activist and anarchist principles their mission is to turn everybody's favourite social drink into a truly, social drink.

La Subersiva

  • Release: November 2022
  • Languages: Catalan
  • Subtitles: English, Dutch
  • Produced by: OTOXO Productions
  • Directed by: Sita van Hasselt

Synopsis:

Born eight years ago out the desire to rid themselves of their dependence on big, capatalist beer brands, Barcelona's "social movements" joint forces starting brew their own beers. With their by and for principle La Subversiva gained considerable succes, being able to return about 19.000 euros to social projects. However the big decision to go legal has forced them to get a loan which at the moment proves quite the dent in to their income. Will they be able to make it work?

This film was made in collaboration with La Subversiva, find out more on subversiva.coop!

Bicibús

Bicibús is a social documentary produced by OTOXO Productions. The film follows a group of parents and volunteers in Barcelona's Eixample neighboorhood as they set out to reclaim the streets from heavy traffic. Watch how Barcelona cracks a smile as the joyful bicibús passes by.

Bicibús: Pedals Endavant

  • Release: Oktober 2022
  • Running time:
  • Languages: Spanish, Catalan
  • Subtitles: English
  • Produced by: OTOXO Productions
  • Directed by: John English, Tom Garner, Rosie Williams, Jenny Dinwoodie, Esme Warren, Caitlin Long, Andy Perez, Clara Cabajo, Katja Kuivanen, Oliver Gibel, Mattia Bullo, Sofia Luz, Alejandra Angulo Alonso, Anya Field, Andra Ion & Sita van Hasselt

Synopsis:

OTOXO Productions' 2022 documentary, 'Bicibús'. The film follows a group of Barcelona parents and children who have joined together to create a stronger community and safer bicycling conditions for the city - starting a local bicibus. The parade of cycling families take the streets every Friday morning, creating a joyful and eco-friendly route to Barcelona elementary schools.
